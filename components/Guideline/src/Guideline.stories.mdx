# @doc-blocks/guideline

A component to display the `Do`'s and `Don't`s of you component.

The `Do` guideline should show the right way to use the component.,

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Do">
    <Do label="Keep button text to 1-2 words">
      <Button>Click me</Button>
    </Do>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

The `Don't` guideline should show the wrong way to use the component.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Dont">
    <Dont label="Have buttons with more than 2 words">
      <Button>Click me to do the thing that is a</Button>
    </Dont>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

Used with `@cgds/row` you can display a pair of `Do/Don't` on one line.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="A Row of Do/Dont">
    <Row>
      <Do label="Keep button text to 1-2 words">
        <Button>Click me</Button>
      </Do>
      <Dont label="Have buttons with more than 2 words">
        <Button>Click me to do the thing that is a</Button>
      </Dont>
    </Row>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
